# Bank Marketing Campaign Analysis

Business Analytics project that analyzes telemarketing campaigns for term deposit subscriptions using descriptive and diagnostic analytics.

This project aims to identify customer segments with the highest subscription potential, evaluate the effectiveness of telemarketing strategies, and provide actionable business recommendations supported by data.

---

## Project Background

Banks rely heavily on telemarketing campaigns to promote term deposit products. However, contacting every customer results in low marketing efficiency because only a small percentage of customers eventually subscribe.

This project analyzes historical marketing campaign data to answer three business questions:

1. What is the overall success rate of the telemarketing campaign?
2. Who are the customers most likely to subscribe to a term deposit?
3. Are current telemarketing efforts being allocated efficiently?

The insights generated are intended to support better customer targeting and campaign optimization.

---

## Objectives

- Measure telemarketing campaign performance
- Identify high-potential customer segments
- Analyze factors affecting campaign success
- Evaluate telemarketing efficiency
- Provide business recommendations based on data

## Key Findings

- Overall campaign conversion rate is **11.27%**.
- Customers aged **60+**, **students**, **retired customers**, and customers with **university degrees** show relatively higher subscription rates.
- Cellular communication significantly outperforms telephone communication.
- Previous campaign success strongly increases the likelihood of future subscription.
- Previous customer interactions are associated with higher conversion rates.
- Campaign timing also influences marketing performance.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Prioritize high-potential customer segments.
- Optimize communication channels by prioritizing cellular contact.
- Utilize previous campaign outcomes for customer prioritization.
- Improve campaign planning using historical performance.
- Adopt a hybrid marketing strategy combining telemarketing and Relationship Managers.
- Develop a data-driven customer segmentation framework.
